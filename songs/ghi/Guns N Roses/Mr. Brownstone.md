Mr. Brownstone  
  
  *This is a transcription to Mr. Brownstone by Guns N' Roses. It is how I*  
  *play it on my acoustic, but it's just like the electric version minus wah*  
  *wah. Playing acoustic tunes on one guitar doesn't lend well to playing*  
  *the solo (from thick, full sound to sparse sound doesn't go so well). For*  
  *the first half of the solo, I play the rhythm part. The second half of the*  
  *solo is very full sounding; enough so that you can play it and it won't*  
  *sound empty coming off the rhythm. A note with a lower case b over it*  
  *means to bend the note up a half step; a note with an upper case B over*  
  *it means to bend the note a full step. A sequence like this 6b7d6 means*  
  *play the 6th fret, bend up 1/2 step to 7th, the release the bend to 6th.*  
  *During the solo you'll see stuff like this: [b d]*  
    [6 6]  
  *This means the same thing as 6b7d6 (I wanted to keep the solo on 80 cols)*  
  *If I made errors, let me know.*
