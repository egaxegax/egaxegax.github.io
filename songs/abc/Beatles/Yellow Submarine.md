Yellow Submarine  
  *(Lennon/McCartney)*  
  
  *I made this version of Yellow Submarine from the way the*  
  *piano version sounded in The Complete Beatles, I have no*  
  *idea how similar it is to the original version. But it*  
  *works for me!*  
  
  *All the notes and chords that fall on the off beat should*  
  *have a dotted rhythm, i.e. instead of two eighth notes play*  
  *a dotted eighth note and a sixteenth note. If you've ever*  
  *heard the song this rhythm should be obvious. Make sure you*  
  *look for the ^ marks to show the beats, don't go by the*  
  *spacing.*  
  
  *I haven't been able to come up with a good rhythm for the*  
  *chorus, so I encourage you to be creative rather than*  
  *following my boring strum pattern.*
