  Permanent Vacation

  *can be fairly duplicated with basic open chords. Most of the*  
  *song is the following riff of ***D*** and ***A***:*

  E ---------------------------------------------

  G ----2-2-0-2----------------------------------  
  D ----2-2-0-2----------------------------------  
  A ----0-0-0-0----------------------------------  
  E --4------------------------------------------]
