  *A Change Of Seasons is one of my favourite DT songs, and I always wanted to play*  
  *it with my band. But, obviously, there was a little problem. I'm the owner of a Iba-*  
  *nez RG Prestige, one of the best guitars in the world, but it "only" has six strings,*  
  *and, as you know, ACOS is written for a seven string guitar. Well, I thought it was*  
  *a cool job to translate it for six-string guitars and, believe me, it works! With a 24*  
  *fret guitar you can play the whole song, and if you only got a 22 fret guitar you will*  
  *only have to modify very few things (a couple of runs in the guitar solos).*  
  *So I think this will be very useful for most guitarists, specially for beginners.*  
  *The method I used is to transpose the song up 5 half-tones (one string), so don't*  
  *forget that the key of the song has changed from Bm to Em, and, therefore the notes*  
  *are not the same.*  
  *In this file, I've just tabbed the first part of the song. If someone find this*  
  *tab useful, please contact me and I will tab the whole song.*
