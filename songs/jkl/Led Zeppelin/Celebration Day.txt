  *(Jones/Page/Plant)*  
  
  *Celebration Day is a cool song that's easy to play if you*  
  *practice it for a while. Make sure you listen to it a few times before*  
  *you attempt to play it. The entire song is in 4/4 so it should be easy to*  
  *keep up. I think this song is in A.*  
  *Fiz tabbed the intro and retabbed the main riff. The A string works as*  
  *a kind of drone throughout the whole song, except for the chorus.*  
  *Let it ring wherever sounds good and try to mute the E string with your*  
  *thumb. There are guitar overdubs in the song, so it's impossible to play*  
  *it exactly just with one guitar. In the chorus the feeling is given by*  
  *the bass lines, it'd be nice if someone could tab them.*  
  *[|] = not a standard bar line; just used to separate music*  
  *~ = vibrato or held note*  
  *x = percussive note with no pitch; muted note*  
  *. = staccato; mute the note immediately after striking it*  
  *h = hammer on*  
  *p = pull off*  
  *s = slide*  
  *^ = downward stroke*  
  *v = upward stroke*  
  *[o]  [\_]*
